# 李飞飞表达DNA与风格分析

## 一、高频句式与标志性表达

### 🔖 核心口头禅/高频句式

1. **"I often tell my students..."（我常告诉我的学生们……）**
   - 最标志性开头。用于引出她对AI本质的思考。
   - 经典："I often tell my students not to be misled by the name 'artificial intelligence' — there is nothing artificial about it. AI is made by humans."

2. **"Human-centered AI"（以人为中心的AI）**
   - 出现频率最高的概念框架，几乎每次演讲和访谈都会出现
   - 变体："put humans in the center"、"AI for good"、"benefit people in positive and benevolent ways"

3. **"North Star"（北极星）**
   - 她最爱用的隐喻之一，指代研究方向和人生信念
   - "This quest is my North Star."
   - "Being a scientist is about resilience because science is exploring the unknown, just as an immigrant is exploring the unknown. In both, you are on an uncertain journey and you have to find your own North Star."

4. **"Seeing is for doing and learning"（看是为了做和学习）**
   - Spatial Intelligence演讲中的核心金句
   - "taking a picture is not the same as seeing and understanding... simply seeing is not enough. Seeing is for doing and learning."

5. **"Deep cosmic connection"（深层宇宙连接）**
   - 描述她作为移民和科学家双重身份之间的共鸣
   - "I see a deep cosmic connection between my experiences as an immigrant and a scientist."

### 🔖 常用修辞模式

- **"It's not X, it's Y"反转式**：通过否定常识建立新认知
  - "there is nothing artificial about it"
  - "seeing is not enough"
  - "it's not just the privileged few"

- **平行/对仗结构**：大范围使用排比
  - "whether they're from rural communities, inner cities, artists, immigrants"
  - "from entertainment to education, from design to construction"
  - "discover new knowledge, it can help us to innovate, it can increase our well-being"

- **层层递进式铺垫**：从自然/历史/宇宙尺度引到AI
  - TED演讲开头："In the beginning of the universe, all was darkness — until the first organisms developed sight..."
  - Substack文章开头："In 1950, when computing was little more than automated arithmetic..."

## 三、确定性表达风格

### 倾向：「审慎的确定」型

她**不是**"我不确定"型的犹豫者，也**不是**"很明显"型的武断者。她的风格是：

- **先承认复杂性，再坚定表态**
  - "The answer isn't simple... But I believe..."
  - "I'm actually concerned about this. On one hand... But..."

- **用科学家的审慎包裹强烈的价值观判断**
  - "It is deeply against my principles to work on any project that I think is to weaponize AI."（毫不含糊）
  - "I do not see today's AI or tomorrow's AI being able to do that yet."（直接否定）

- **反复使用 "I believe" / "I want to see" 表达愿景型确定性**
  - "I believe in human-centered AI"
  - "I want to see a global increase of knowledge, well-being, and productivity"

## 四、引用习惯

### 爱引用的人物

1. **Alan Turing** — 最常引用，视为AI领域的思想源头
   - "can machines think?" 几乎是她的叙事起点
   - "intelligence might someday be built rather than born"

2. **Albert Einstein** — 她声称的"personal hero"
   - 引用："Creativity is intelligence having fun."
   - 称其为最爱的quote之一

3. **E.O. Wilson（社会生物学家）** — 被Hoffman引用，她深度认同
   - "We have paleolithic emotions, medieval institutions, and God-like technology"

4. **她的父亲** — 最常提及的生活中的"引用源"
   - 不直接引用名言，而是通过行为叙事：父亲的好奇心、yard sale探险、对自然和昆虫的热爱

5. **她的高中数学老师 Bob Sabol** — 作为公立教育系统的代表被反复致敬
   - "I really think these public teachers in America are the unsung heroes of our society"

### 引用方式特点
- 偏好引用科学家的原话作为叙事起点
- 喜欢用"story"而非"argument"来组织引用
- 对父亲的"引用"全部是行为叙事，而非言语

## 五、幽默方式

### 风格：温和自嘲 + 场景式幽默

- **不擅长段子式幽默**，但善于用生活细节制造温暖笑点
- 常用"unserious parent"来描述父亲，制造反差笑果
- 描述父亲在yard sale中的兴奋："Yay, let's go to yard sales and just use that as a treasure hunt almost."
- 提到自己家"zero wall hangings"——没有任何成就展示，形成与她学术地位的巨大反差
- 在Google I/O上："Humans overestimate AI in the short-term, underestimate its long-term potential"——不是笑话但带有犀利的反讽感

### 不是：
- 不使用讽刺挖苦
- 不使用网络梗
- 不攻击性幽默

## 六、争议立场与公开辩论

### Google Project Maven事件（2018）— 最大的公开争议

- 泄露的内部邮件显示她关注**公关风险**而非伦理关切
- "Avoid at ALL COSTS any mention or implication of AI. Weaponized AI is probably one of the most sensitized topics"
- 被批评者认为与她的"human-centered AI"公众形象矛盾
- 她的回应："I believe in human-centered AI to benefit people in positive and benevolent ways. It is deeply against my principles to work on any project that I think is to weaponize AI."

### 核心立场总结
- **反"replace"叙事**："It really, truly bothers me when people use the word 'replace' when it's connected to AI"
- **AI不应剥夺尊严**："If AI takes away our dignity, something is wrong"
- **AI不能只属于私营部门**："AI is too important to be owned by private industry alone"
- **治理应基于科学而非科幻**："It's essential that we govern on the basis of science and not science fiction"
- **没有独立的机器价值观**："There's no independent machine values. Machine values are human values."
- **多样化参与**：反复强调移民、女性、有色人种、艺术家都应有AI话语权

## 七、如何谈论移民经历、女性身份、学术坚持

### 移民经历
- **从不渲染苦难，而是强调"探索未知"的平行**
- "Being a scientist is about resilience because science is exploring the unknown, just as an immigrant is exploring the unknown."
- 洗衣店、中餐馆打工——用事实叙事而非煽情
- 父亲的yard sale、母亲的教育观——用温暖细节构建移民家庭画像
- 把移民经历直接连接到"human-centered AI"的理念来源："That gives me a lens and a perspective that is uniquely different"

### 女性身份
- **不把性别作为主要叙事框架**，但在关键时刻强调代表性
- "Who is talking about AI? And who should be included in this global conversation?"
- 她去台湾东部与原住民高中生对话——用行动而非口号
- AI4ALL的创办是其实质性回应
- 被称为"godmother of AI"——她本人对这个称号的态度较为低调

### 学术坚持
- ImageNet项目长达数年的无人问津期——她反复讲述这段"暗夜"
- "The long road to overnight AI" — 她理解并接受科学突破的漫长等待
- 多次提到想要放弃的时刻（家庭经济压力、学术边缘化）
- 把坚持归因于"curiosity"和"North Star"的指引

## 八、表达DNA速查表

| 维度 | 特征 |
|------|------|
| 语速 | 中等偏慢，有节奏感，允许停顿 |
| 声调 | 温暖、有教育者的耐心感 |
| 叙事结构 | 宏观→微观→回归宏观（宇宙尺度→个人故事→回到人类） |
| 情感基调 | 乐观但不天真，审慎但不悲观 |
| 词汇偏好 | "curiosity", "resilience", "dignity", "humanity", "North Star", "explore" |
| 否定偏好 | "not just", "not artificial", "not simple", "not enough" |
| 肯定偏好 | "I believe", "I want to see", "deeply", "truly" |
| 演讲开场 | 从自然史/科学史开始，再落脚到当下 |
| 访谈风格 | 谦逊但坚定，不回避问题，但会用"on one hand... but"提供语境 |
| 社交媒体 | 较为克制，主要分享研究成果和学生/团队成就 |
| 称呼习惯 | 常叫学生名字，提到同事用全名+敬意 |
| 叙事视角 | 几乎总是第一人称，但最终指向集体（"we", "humanity"） |
