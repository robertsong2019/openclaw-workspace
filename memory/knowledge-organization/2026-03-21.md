#### 1.3 Anthropic Building Effective Agents

**文章**: https://www.anthropic.com/engineering/building-effective-agents
**HN**: 544 points

**核心观点**:
> "The most successful implementations weren't using complex frameworks. Instead, they were building with simple, composable patterns."

**五种工作流模式**:
1. **Prompt Chaining** 🔗 - 顺序步骤
2. **Routing** 🛤️ - 分类路由
3. **Parallelization** ⚡ - 并行聚合
4. **Orchestrator-Workers** 🎼 - 动态分解
5. **Evaluator-Optimizer** 🔄 - 迭代改进

**三个核心原则**:
1. **Maintain Simplicity** ✂️ - 不要过度设计
2. **Prioritize Transparency** 🔍 - 明确展示规划步骤
3. **Craft ACI** 🛠️ - 投入与 HCI 同样的精力

**对 AI Agent 编程的启示**:
- 简单 > 复杂
- 从简单开始，只在需要时增加复杂性
- 透明度是信任的基础
- ACI 设计与 HCI 同等重要
